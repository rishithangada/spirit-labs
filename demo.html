Walk-forward validated trading signals

Strategy-003 was validated on 269 out-of-sample trades with a +2.27% average return per trade and 53.9% win rate. The system generates, logs, and tracks every signal automatically.

  • Regime gate: SPY vs MA50 prevents entries in risk-off markets
  • Alpha conviction score: 8 signals weighted 0–100
  • Catalyst filter: drops Cramer/listicle headlines before judging
  • GEX context: reduces confidence when SPY is gamma-pinned
  • Paper ledger: every verdict tracked, outcomes scored at 30 bars
  • Telegram: real-time alerts, paper order via /confirm

  • Telegram interface: run any agent from your phone
